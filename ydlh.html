You Don't Live Here by Robyn Schneider

I was originally interested in this book as it covered both death and was hinted at being gay. It covers grief in a way that made me cry, reminding me of my own experience with grief. The relationship aspect got on my nerves, but that’s typical for a young adults romance novel. It was good to listen to in the background while I do other things, as I wasn’t totally invested in the book.
