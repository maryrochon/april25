Survive The Night by Riley Sager

After getting past the first 15% of the book, I WAS INVESTED. I wanted to know if he was the killer or not. And if Charlie would be able to figure out what really happened the night of Maddy’s murder. I listened to this book all the way through, and couldn’t wait to find out more. I was not expecting the two twists at the end. I would definitely listen to other books written by this author and will 100% be further exploring the thriller genre.
