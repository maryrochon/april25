The Subtweet by Vivek Shraya

This short 5 hour audiobook, talks about pinning two woman against each other especially in the music industry. Having the setting of the book in the Toronto helped me get more invested in the book sooner. Later there is a case of miscommunication, or more like a broken phone that made me so irritated. I don’t like this cliché, and it was the reason for all the problems. Yet I was still very invested in the book.
