The Break By Katherena Vermette

This book is told from multiple different perspectives, some were more connected to the main plot line than others. It is harder to follow who’s perspective it is when I’m listening to audiobooks, especially at 2.55 speed, and it changes frequently. The perspectives that didn’t progress the plot were less interesting to me. However I was invested in figuring out who was attacked and who was the attacker from the start. It took till the near the end for me figure out how everyone is related to each other.
